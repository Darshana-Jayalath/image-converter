Our Story

ImageSwap was born from a simple frustration: converting image formats shouldn't be complicated, expensive, or require downloading bulky software. Founded in 2025 by Darshana Jayalath, we set out to create the most user-friendly image conversion tool on the web with a clear mission—to provide everyone with fast, reliable, and completely free image conversion services. What started as a personal project has grown into something much bigger. Today, we proudly serve thousands of users worldwide, helping them convert millions of images every month across all formats including JPG, PNG, GIF, BMP, and WEBP. We're constantly innovating and adding new features based on your feedback, ensuring our platform remains the simplest, fastest, and most accessible image conversion solution available. Our commitment to quality, security, and user privacy remains unwavering as we continue to empower creators, businesses, and individuals around the globe to work with images effortlessly, without any technical barriers or financial constraints.
